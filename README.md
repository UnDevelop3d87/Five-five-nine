# 🌻 Fresno Resource Hub™ / Five59ineHub™

**Community Based Resource Center for EVERYONE**

A free, mobile-friendly community resource directory connecting
Fresno County residents to the help they need — food, housing, jobs,
utility assistance, legal aid, mental health services, and more.

🔗 **Live site:** https://fivefiveninehub.netlify.app

---

## What It Does

Fresno Resource Hub puts 79+ local organizations and 28+ community
events in one place, organized by category, with tap-to-call,
tap-to-email, and direct website links. No account required. No cost.
No barriers.

### Categories include:
- 🍎 Food & Pantries
- 🏘️ Housing & Shelter
- 💡 Utility Assistance
- 💼 Jobs & Training
- ❤️ Health Services
- 🧠 Mental Health
- 👶 Children & Families
- 👴 Senior Services
- ⚖️ Legal Aid
- 🚨 Emergency Services
- 🪶 Tribal Nations
- ⛪ Places of Worship
- 🕊️ Funeral & Memorial
- 🏛️ City Hall & Civic
- 🎉 Events & Entertainment

---

## Features

- 📱 Mobile-responsive, works on any device
- 🌐 Multi-language support (Google Translate)
- 🔍 Search across all resources
- ⭐ Save favorite resources
- 📲 Add to Home Screen (works like an app)
- 💛 Community donation support

---

## Built With

- HTML, CSS, JavaScript (single-file static site)
- Hosted on Netlify

---

## Mission

Connect every Fresno County resident to the resources they need,
with zero friction. No accounts. No income verification to browse.
No judgment. Just clear, organized, up-to-date information.

Built by a Fresno resident, for Fresno residents. 🌻
